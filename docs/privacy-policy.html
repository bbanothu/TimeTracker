Privacy Policy

TimeTracker (package: com.time_tracker.app) · Last updated: July 5, 2026

TimeTracker (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) helps you track how you spend your time using tags, saved places, goals, and statistics. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Who operates the app

TimeTracker is operated by the developer of this application. If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at bbanothu1997@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information — email address and password when you register. You may optionally add a first name, last name, and profile photo.
  • Time tracking data — activity tags, timer start/end times, optional session notes, and whether a session was started manually or by a saved place.
  • Location data — with your permission, the app uses your device location to show your position on the map, save places, auto-start tracking when you enter a saved place, and optionally record GPS coordinates when you stop a session. Background location is used only if you enable automatic place-based tracking.
  • Saved places — names, map coordinates, radius, and linked tags for places you configure.
  • Goals and statistics — daily targets and progress derived from your time entries.
  • Social features — if you use Friends, we store friend requests and friendships linked to account email addresses.
  • Device data — push notification tokens (mobile) so we can alert you when you arrive at a saved place, and basic app preferences such as theme settings stored on your device.

How we use information

  • Provide sign-in, sync, and core time-tracking features
  • Run automatic tracking at saved places when you opt in
  • Show stats, goals, history, and maps
  • Enable optional friend features and profile display
  • Improve reliability and fix errors

We do not sell your personal information.

Where data is stored

Account and synced data are stored in a Supabase cloud database and file storage service operated by Supabase, Inc. On mobile, data is also stored locally on your device in SQLite and synced when you are online. Profile photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

Data sharing

We share data only as needed to operate the app:

  • Service providers — Supabase (authentication, database, and file storage)
  • Friends you connect with — accepted friends can view your statistics as designed in the app; they cannot edit your data
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect rights and safety

Your choices and controls

  • Deny or revoke location and notification permissions in your device settings
  • Disable individual saved places or background auto-tracking in the app
  • Export your time entries as CSV from Profile
  • Delete individual entries from History
  • Change your password or sign out at any time
  • Request account or data deletion by emailing bbanothu1997@gmail.com

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. If you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize your account data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

TimeTracker is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International users

Your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
